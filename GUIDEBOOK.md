## 📦 Sanctum Items

These essential resources and artifacts form the foundation of your journey within the Sanctum.

### Core Resources

* **Gold Coins:** The primary currency. Earned through sessions, [Dungeons](#-sanctum-map), and [Quests](#-sanctum-map). Used in the [Merchant Outpost](#-sanctum-map) for items and Gacha.
* **XP (Experience):** Represents your overall growth. Earn **XP** to level up, which unlocks rewards and [Talent Scrolls](#-talent-system).

Resources dedicated to your skill development:

* **Talent Scrolls:** Rare points used exclusively to unlock powerful passive traits in the [Talent Tree](#-talent-system).
* **Talent Shards:** Fragments of potential. Collect 3 shards to automatically forge 1 full **Talent Scroll**. Sometimes they drop together with items.

### Advanced Items

Powerful artifacts that can significantly alter your progress. Obtained mainly from the [Merchant's shop](#-sanctum-map) or Dungeon rewards:

* **Death Defying Medal:** Automatically consumed to prevent a [Dungeon Goal](#-sanctum-map) from failing if you miss a deadline, preserving your progress.
* **Double XP Card:** When activated in your [Vault](#-sanctum-map), temporarily doubles all **Experience Points** earned from sessions and quests.
* **Double Gold Card:** When activated in your [Vault](#-sanctum-map), temporarily doubles all **Gold Coins** earned, accelerating your wealth.

> **Pro Tip: Item Usage**  
> Visit the Vault and click "Use Item" on consumable cards to activate their effects. They usually expire at the end of the day.

## 🪙 Gold Coins
*Economy & Wealth*

Gold is the primary currency within the Sanctum. It is used to purchase items from the [Merchant's Outpost](#-sanctum-map) and roll the Gacha.

### How to Acquire
* **❖** Complete sessions using the [Timer](#-sanctum-map)
* **❖** Clear rooms in [Dungeons](#-sanctum-map)
* **❖** Complete [Quests](#-sanctum-map) & [Achievements](#-sanctum-map)

> **Pro Tip: Customization**  
> You can adjust how much Gold you earn and customize level-up gifts!
> * **Go to Settings > Developer:** Adjust base Gold rates and multipliers.
> * **Go to Settings > Level Rewards:** Add or modify Gold gifts upon leveling up.

## 🎯 XP & Leveling
*Growth & Progression*

Gain Experience Points (XP) to level up. Leveling up can reward you with items, [Talent Scrolls](#-talent-system), or Gold.

### How to Acquire
* **❖** Complete focus blocks via the [Timer](#-sanctum-map)
* **❖** Slay monsters in [Dungeons](#-sanctum-map)
* **❖** Claim [Quests](#-sanctum-map) & [Achievements](#-sanctum-map)

> **Pro Tip: Customization**  
> Configure your level-up rewards to match your progression style!
> * **Go to Settings > Developer:** Adjust base XP rates and formula.
> * **Go to Settings > Level Rewards:** Add/remove level-up gifts (e.g., loot).

---

## 🕸️ Talent System
*Unlocking Potential*

The [Talent Tree](#-sanctum-map) allows you to unlock passive bonuses. You need **Talent Scrolls** to unlock nodes.

### Acquiring Talent Scrolls
* **❖ Leveling Up:** Points are granted at specific level milestones.
* **❖ Finding Shards:** Shards are rare drops in [Dungeons](#-sanctum-map) or won from the [Merchant](#-sanctum-map).

> **Pro Tip: Customization**  
> Want more Talent Scrolls? You can set them as rewards for leveling up!
> * **Go to Settings > Level Rewards:** Add Talent Scrolls or Shards as level-up gifts.
